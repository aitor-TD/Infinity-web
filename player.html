What is spatial audio?
Spatial audio was created in 2021 and we bring it to you with this new Spotify version. With Spotify Infinity we wanted to change the way of listening music, it doesnt matter if you are using headphones or speakers, you will be able to use this 360 audio feature.
We wanted to make another version of Spotify for this new audio system, so we can let the users who don't want to use this system have the normal audio mode. If you are going to purchase a plan for this new app and you have a premium plan on the normal Spotify you will have a 30% discount.
